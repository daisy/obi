Audio Processing

Provides access to the new and enhanced audio processing functionality of Obi. It has a submenu listing the following functions:

  • Change Volume: Increases or decreases the volume of the audio contained in the selected item(s) by a specified rate. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl+Shift+U.
  • Fade In: Increases the volume of the audio contained in the selected item(s) gradually. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl+Shift+I.
  • Fade Out: Decreases the volume of the audio contained in the selected item(s) gradually. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl+Shift+O.
  • Normalize: Applies the constant amount of gain to the audio of the selected item(s) so that the average and peak amplitudes are within the threshold of the audio file. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl+Shift+Z.
  • Speech Rate: Increases/Decreases the speech rate of audio contained in the selected item.
  • Noise Reduction: Reduces the noise in the audio content of selected items. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl+Shift+Y.
  • Audio Mixer: Mixes audio of the selected phrase with audio from external files. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl+Shift+X.
