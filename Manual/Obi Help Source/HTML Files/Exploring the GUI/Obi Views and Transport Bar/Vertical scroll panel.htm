Vertical Scroll Panel

 

Obi offers a set of scroll buttons via a vertical scroll panel. These buttons let you scroll vertically through contents of the section currently displayed in the Content view.

 

The vertical scroll panel, depicted in the figure below, is positioned at the rightmost edge of the Content view.

Figure: Vertical scroll panel
