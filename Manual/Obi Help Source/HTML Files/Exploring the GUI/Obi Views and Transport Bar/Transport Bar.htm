Transport Bar

 

Transport Bar can be defined as a control panel offering controls for working with audio in Obi. It provides the controls for playing or recording audio, audio monitoring, and navigation.

 

The controls offered by the Transport bar can be categorized as follows:

  • Audio and navigation controls
  • Textual peak meter
  • Time display 

 

The Transport Bar can be in various states. Behavior of some of the controls may vary according to these states.

 

Show/Hide Transport Bar

The Transport Bar can be shown or hidden by selecting View->Show Transport Bar from the menu or pressing Ctrl+Alt+B. It is shown by default.
