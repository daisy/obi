Navigating among items

 

You may use the following keyboard shortcuts for navigating among sections, phrases and phrase separators in the Content view:

  • To move to the first section in the Content View, press Ctrl+Home, and to move to the last section, press Ctrl+End.
  • To move to the next section, press Ctrl+Shift+Down Arrow and to move to the previous section, press Ctrl+Shift+Up Arrow.

If you are at a section label, to move to the first phrase of the section, press Right Arrow.

  • If you are currently at a phrase or phrase separator, press Ctrl+Shift+Up Arrow to move to the section label. Pressing Ctrl+Shift+Down Arrow will take you to the section label of the section below.
  • You can move to the next phrase by pressing Right Arrow and to the previous phrase by using the Left Arrow.
  • To move to a phrase separator after the current phrase, press Ctrl+Right Arrow. Similarly, to move to a phrase separator before the current phrase, press Ctrl+Left Arrow.
  • When you are currently on a phrase separator, to move to the next one, press Ctrl+Right Arrow, and to move to a previous one, press Ctrl+Left Arrow.
  • To move to a phrase before the current phrase separator, press Left Arrow. To move to a phrase after the current phrase separator, press Right Arrow.
  • To move to the first phrase of the section, press Home, and to move to the last phrase of the section, press End.
  • Use Up Arrow to move to the first phrase of the preceding row and Down Arrow to move to the first phrase in the row below.
  • Press Page Up to move up one screen, and Page Down to move down one screen.

Press Ctrl+Page Down to move to the next page phrase, and Ctrl+Page Up to move to the previous page phrase.

  • You can move to a phrase holding a special role after the current phrase by pressing F4. To move to a phrase with a special role before the current phrase, press Shift+F4.
  • To move to the next Todo phrase, press Ctrl+F9, and to move to the Todo phrase before the current phrase, press Ctrl+Shift+F9.
  • To move to the next empty phrase in the project, press Ctrl+Alt+F4.

 

You may also traverse all the phrases of the section one by one using the keystrokes Tab or Shift+Tab.

 

For example:

If you are at the section label, pressing Tab will take you to the first phrase. If you press Tab again, the keyboard focus will move to the second phrase. Now if there are only two phrases in the section, this keystroke will take you to the section label again.

Conversely, the keystrokes Shift+Tab will move you backward in the section. This means, it will move to the first phrase from the second one, and then to the section label.

 

If the Play on navigate option is turned on, Obi starts the playback of the item that receives the focus.
