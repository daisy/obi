Assign role

 

Assigns a particular role to a phrase in the project.

 

This menu item has a submenu containing the following items:

  • Plain: Clear the role assigned previously.
  • Heading: Contains the audio pertaining to the heading of the section.
  • Page: Contains the page number.
  • Silence: Contains the silence audio of recording room.
  • List of skippable notes: The list of skippable notes such as annotation, footnote, etc.
  • (New custom role): You can also assign custom role to the phrase by providing the custom class name.
