Editing Audio

 

Obi provides various commands for editing audio that can either be applied on a phrase or an audio selection. This topic will explain how to cut, copy, paste or delete an audio selection. Follow the links below to learn more about editing commands:

Cut/Copy

For cutting or copying the audio, you first have to make an audio selection.

Then, you may cut the selected audio by pressing Ctrl+X or by clicking Edit->Cut on the menu.

Or, copy the selected audio by pressing Ctrl+C or by clicking Edit->Copy on the menu.

 

Obi stores the audio you just cut or copied in the clipboard.

 

Paste 

You can paste the audio from the clipboard by pressing Ctrl+V or by clicking Edit->Paste on the menu.

The audio will be pasted according to the following rules:

  • If you set the audio cursor on the waveform of the phrase, the audio from the clipboard will be pasted there.
  • If you make an audio selection, the selection will be replaced by the audio from the clipboard.
  • If you select a phrase, the audio will be appended to the phrase.
  • If you select a phrase separator, a new phrase will be created in its position and audio will be filled in it.
  • If you select a section, Obi will create a new phrase at the end of the section and fill in the audio from the clipboard into it.

 

Deleting the selected audio

An audio selection can be deleted by clicking Edit->Delete on the menu or pressing Del.
