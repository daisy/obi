Exporting and Validating

This is the last step of creating an accessible book. You must ensure the following things before going further:

  • All the contents of the book have been entered correctly.
  • The structure of the book is in sync with the physical book.
  • Metadata entries are filled properly.

Now, you will have to perform an export operation to publish the DAISY/EPUB 3 book.

But before distribution, the contents of the DTB must be validated. Obi provides validators to check your exported DTB for compliance with DAISY/EPUB specifications.
