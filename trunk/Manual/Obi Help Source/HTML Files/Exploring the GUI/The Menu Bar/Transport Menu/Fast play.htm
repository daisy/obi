Fast play

 

Changes the rate at which audio is being played. This command is different from the Fast Forward command, as it will play the audio at faster rate without any gaps. But the pitch may be distorted. To know how to maintain the pitch, refer to the topic Fast Play.

 

It has a submenu containing the following items:

·        Normal Speed: Restore the normal speed for playback. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl+0.

·        Normal speed with Elapse Back: Restore normal speed after playing a small portion of audio before the audio cursor.

·        Speed Up: Increase the rate of playback. Obi picks up the next value from the combo box Fast Play Rate of Transport Bar, and starts playing the audio at that rate. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl++.

·        Slow Down: Decreases the rate at which audio is being played. Obi picks up the previous value from the combo box Fast Play Rate of Transport Bar, and starts playing the audio at that rate. The keyboard shortcut is Ctrl+-.

 

Obi also provides single key shortcuts for these commands, listed below:

·         Normal speed: D

·         Normal speed with elapse back: E

·         Speed up: F

·         Slow Down: S

Note: The single key shortcuts can only be used in Content View.
