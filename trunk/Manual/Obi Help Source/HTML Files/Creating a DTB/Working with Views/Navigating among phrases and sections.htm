Navigating among items

 

You can use the following keyboard shortcuts while navigating among sections, phrases and phrase separators:

·        To move to the next section, press Down Arrow and to move to the previous section, press Up Arrow.

·        To move to the first section in the Content View, press Ctrl+Home, and to move to the last section, press Ctrl+End.

·        If you are at a section label, to move to the first phrase of the section, press Right Arrow.

·        If you are currently at a phrase or phrase separator, press Up Arrow to move to the section label. Pressing Down Arrow will take you to the section label of the section below.

·        You can move to the next phrase by pressing Right Arrow and to the previous phrase by using the Left Arrow.

·        To move to a phrase separator after the current phrase, press Ctrl+Right Arrow. Similarly, to move to a phrase separator before the current phrase, press Ctrl+Left Arrow.

·        When you are currently on a phrase separator, to move to the next one, press Ctrl+Right Arrow, and to move to a previous one, press Ctrl+Left Arrow.

·        To move to a phrase before the current phrase separator, press Left Arrow. To move to a phrase after the current phrase separator, press Right Arrow.

·        To move to the first phrase of the section, press Home, and to move to the last phrase of the section, press End.

 

Note: You can traverse all the items (sections and phrases) one by one by using Tab or Shift+Tab.

 

For example:

If you are at the second section, pressing Tab will take you to the first phrase of the second section. Pressing Tab again will take you to the second phrase of the second section. Now, if there are only two phrases in the section, pressing Tab will take you to the third section.

Conversely, if you are at the second section and press Shift+Tab, Obi will select the last phrase of the first section. This means if there are three phrases in the first section, the third phrase of the first section will be highlighted. Pressing Shift+Tab again will move the focus to the second phrase of the first section.

If the Play on navigate option is set, Obi starts the playback of the item that receives the focus. What will be played depends on what gets selected:

·        If a phrase gets selected, the audio contained in the phrase will be played.

·        If the section label gets selected, the audio of the heading phrase will be played. If there is no heading in the section, audio content from the first phrase will be played.

 

To turn this option off or on, click Transport->Navigation->Play On Navigate on the menu.
