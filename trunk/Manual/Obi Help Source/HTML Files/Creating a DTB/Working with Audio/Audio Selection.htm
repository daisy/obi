Audio Selection

 

Audio can be selected during playback using keyboard shortcuts. You can also select the audio from the waveform beneath the phrase label using mouse.

 

Using keyboard shortcuts:

1.      Start playing the audio of the phrase by pressing Space.

2.      Press Space again to pause at the position from where you want to select.

3.      To mark the beginning of the audio selection, press Ctrl+ [.

4.      Resume the playback by pressing Space.

5.      When the content of your selection has been played, pause again by pressing Space. Please note that this step may be omitted.

6.      To mark the end of the selection, press Ctrl+].

 

Using mouse:

1.      Left click on a position on the waveform to mark the beginning of the selection.

2.      Shift+Left click on a position after that in the same waveform to mark the end of the selection.

The alternative method is listed below:

1.      Place the mouse pointer on a position on the waveform from where you want to start the selection. To mark the beginning of the selection, press the left mouse button.

2.      Drag the mouse horizontally on the waveform and release where you want to mark the end of the selection.

 

Using Transport Bar

1.      Start playing the audio by clicking the Play Button.

2.      Click the Pause button at a position from where you want to select the audio.

3.      To mark the beginning of the audio selection, press Ctrl+ [, or click Phrases->Audio Selection->Begin Audio Selection on the menu.

4.      Resume the playback by clicking the Play button again.

5.      When the content of your selection has been played, pause again by clicking the Pause button. Please note that this step may be omitted.

6.      To mark the end of the selection, press Ctrl+], or click Phrases->Audio Selection->End Audio Selection on the menu.

Obi provides some other commands for facilitating selection of required audio content. They are listed as follows:

  • The entire audio content of the phrase can be selected by pressing a single key - A.
  • The audio content from the beginning of the phrase to the audio cursor can be selected by pressing Shift+Home.
  • The audio content from the audio cursor to the end of the phrase can be selected by pressing Shift+End.

 

Please note that the audio selection can be deselected by pressing Esc.  When you deselect, its container element gets selected. This means, if an audio selection is deselected, the phrase containing it gets selected. Similarly, if you press Esc, when a phrase was selected, its enclosing section gets selected.

 

Remarks:

When you select a phrase, its corresponding audio also gets selected.

Similarly, when you select a section, audio contained in all the phrases of the section will get selected.
